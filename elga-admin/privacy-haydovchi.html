ELGA Haydovchi — Privacy Policy (English)

Last updated: June 22, 2026 · App: ELGA Haydovchi (uz.elga.driver)
Operator: "BUSINESS-CENTER-ANGOR" LLC, Angor district, Surxondaryo, Uzbekistan.
Contact: innokasbmarkazi@gmail.com

This is a driver-side taxi application. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

1. Data we collect

  • Location (GPS) — precise location, to find orders, navigate to passengers, and show live position during a trip.
  • Background location — location is sent even when the app is closed or the screen is off, so you can keep receiving orders and the passenger can see your car in real time. Location is shared only while you are "Online"; turning "Offline" stops it.
  • Account data — name, phone number, car model and plate number.
  • Trip data — order addresses, price, distance, status.
  • Device data — device identifier (for security and duplicate-request protection).

2. How we use data

  • Assigning orders and connecting drivers with passengers;
  • Navigation and fare calculation;
  • Safety, fraud prevention, and support;
  • Push notifications.

3. Data sharing

Your location is shown only to the relevant passenger during an active order. We do not sell your data. Data is used solely to provide the service (server, push service).

4. Retention & security

Data is stored on a secure server (HTTPS). We do not retain long-term location history — only current/recent trips.

5. Your rights

You may request deletion of your account and data by emailing innokasbmarkazi@gmail.com. Requests are handled within 30 days.

6. Children

The app is not intended for persons under 18.

7. Changes

This policy may be updated; the updated date is shown above.
