Buyurtma davomида ism va olib ketish joyi tayinlangan haydovchiga ko'rsatiladi. Biz ma'lumotlaringizni uchinchi tomonlarga sotmaymiz.

ELGA Mijoz — Privacy Policy (English)

Last updated: June 22, 2026 · App: ELGA Mijoz (uz.elga.mijoz)
Operator: "BUSINESS-CENTER-ANGOR" LLC, Angor district, Surxondaryo, Uzbekistan.
Contact: innokasbmarkazi@gmail.com

This app lets passengers order a taxi. This policy explains what data we collect.

1. Data we collect

  • Location (GPS) — to order a taxi, set the pickup point, and show the driver's car on the map. Location is used only while the app is open (no background location).
  • Microphone — only when you tap "voice order", to speak your destination. The audio is delivered to the driver for that order.
  • Account data — name, phone number.
  • Order data — addresses, price, payment method, status.
  • Device data — device identifier (for security and duplicate-order protection).

2. How we use data

  • Creating taxi orders and connecting with a driver;
  • Fare calculation and payment;
  • Safety and support;
  • Push notifications.

3. Data sharing

During an order, your name and pickup location are shown to the assigned driver. We do not sell your data.

4. Retention & security

Data is stored on a secure server (HTTPS). Voice-order audio is used only for that order.

5. Your rights

You may request deletion of your account and data by emailing innokasbmarkazi@gmail.com. Requests are handled within 30 days.

6. Children

The app is not intended for persons under 18.

7. Changes

This policy may be updated; the updated date is shown above.
